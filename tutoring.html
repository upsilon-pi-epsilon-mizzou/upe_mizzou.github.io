Tutoring now available for CS 1050 Students

Upsilon Pi Epsilon is extending its hand towards all CS 1050 students who may be struggling in the various elements of software development. Coding doesn't come easy to everyone and we know there are plenty of vague elements within the field. As such, we are allotting a hour long session every week for those who may need assistance with their assignments and help clear any confusion surrounding related topics.

Details are as follows:

Itinerary: Every Tuesday (Except for the first Tuesday of the month) 5:30 - 6:30 PM

Location: Naka Hall, Room 239

Contact: Sarah Woodridge sawqxd@mail.missouri.edu
